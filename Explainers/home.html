Explainers

If you are anything like me, you learn best by explaining to others. This is a forcing function to get me to explain new material as I am learning it. I seriously doubt it will ever be of use to anybody. If it is, I sincerely pity you. I do hope, however, that there is at least one redeeming feature of these explainers: I try to avoid working backwards as much as I can. Many theorems are simple to prove once you know the conclusion. I have taken at least attempted to get at the important ideas while pretending not to already know them.

Many of the sections are not hyperlinked. That is because they are not yet written, but I want them to be some day.
